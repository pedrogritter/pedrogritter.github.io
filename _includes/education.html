Studying at Restart School

A Dive into Audiovisuals and Multimedia

In 2013, I pursued my passion for cinema by enrolling in an Audiovisuals and Multimedia course at Restart School. This year was incredibly enriching and enjoyable, providing me with a deep understanding of the cinematic arts.

Cinema and TV Theory: I learned about camera angles, scriptwriting, and symbolic techniques, which helped me understand the intricate details of visual storytelling.

Video Post-production: I gained hands-on experience in editing techniques, color correction, and raw data management, enhancing the quality of the content I capture.

Production and Direction: I became proficient in operating video cameras and microphones, setting up multi-camera shoots, and creating the right studio settings with proper lighting.

Though I didn't pursue a career in this field, the experience was immensely rewarding. It deepened my love for cinema and provided me with skills I regularly use in my personal projects, capturing and editing footage from my travels and hobbies with a cinematic touch.

Journey Through University

Earning a Computer Science Degree

From 2014 to 2021, I pursued a degree in Computer Science at the Univesity of Lisbon. This period was crucial in developing my technical skills, critical thinking, and passion for innovation.

Foundations and Practical Applications: The coursework covered fundamental computer science concepts, including algorithms, data structures, and computational theory, essential for problem-solving and efficient programming.

Programming and Software Development: I gained proficiency in Python, Java, and C++, developing real-world applications and gaining hands-on experience in writing, debugging, and optimizing code.

Geographical Information Systems (GIS): Specializing in GIS, I learned to analyze spatial data and create digital maps, which broadened my perspective on using tech to solve real-world problems.

Collaborative Projects: Working on group projects taught me the importance of teamwork, communication, and project management, enhancing my ability to contribute effectively to a team.

Cutting-edge Technology: Exposure to AI, machine learning, and cybersecurity sparked a curiosity that drives me to explore new technological advancements continuously.

Multidisciplinary Education: My degree included complementary courses in Management, Statistics, and GIS, preparing me for advanced studies in fields like Bioinformatics, Data Science, and Information Security.

Skills Acquired: I developed skills in designing, implementing, and managing IT systems, integrating enterprise information systems, and applying information technologies in various fields.

Earning a Computer Science degree was more than an academic achievement; it was a journey of growth and discovery. It equipped me with the skills and mindset to tackle complex problems and innovate solutions, striving to make a positive impact through technology.
